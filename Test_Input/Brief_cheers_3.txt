NEW PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT BRIEF

Project: Clean-Label Vegan Cheese for Baking

Overview:
Seeking a vegan oven cheese preparation with gyros/Mediterranean herb seasoning 
for our health-conscious product line. Must meet strict clean-label requirements.

MANDATORY SPECIFICATIONS:
✓ 100% Vegan
✓ Gluten-Free (certified <20ppm)
✓ Halal Compliant
✓ GMO-Free (Genfrei)
✓ Allergen-Free formulation

FLAVOR & APPEARANCE:
- Savory/würzig taste profile
- Natural coloring only (no artificial colors)
- Natural flavors permitted
- No nature-identical aromas

EXCLUDED INGREDIENTS:
× Sweeteners (keine Süßstoffe)
× Saccharose
× Preservatives
× Artificial colors
× Aspartam
× IBKM

ACCEPTABLE INGREDIENTS:
○ Starch (Stärke)
○ Natural aromas
○ Standard stabilization systems

Category: Zubereitung (Preparation)
Segment: Käse (Cheese)
Type: Standardprodukt
