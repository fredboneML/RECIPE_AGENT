# Customer Brief - Matcha for Quark/Topfen
# Target: Should match MATCHA SIGGIS recipes (Austria)

## Flavor Concept
Matcha preparation for Quark/Topfen application with fruit pieces.

## Fruit Preparation Properties
- pH: 3.3-4.3
- Brix: 40-57°
- Viscosity (60s): 7-10 cm
- With pieces (Stückig)
- Fat level: around 2%
- Dosage: 10%

## Key Requirements
- No preservatives
- No artificial colors
- Natural flavors
- Contains Saccharose
- Starch stabilizer

## Target Market
Austria
